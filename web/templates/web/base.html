Inroduction to Phad paintings

Phad is cloth and wall painting that visually depicts the story of the local hero-god. While the story is narrated using songs and dance, the visual impact provided by the phad. Stories of Pabuji and Devnarayan ji are populer in Rajathan, especially if they are in Phad style.

Phad Painting Training and Research Institute - Chitrashla

Chitrashla is a pioneer institute for training the different styles of paintings of Rajasthan and especially committed for Phad painting (Mewar Style).Chitrashala has given many internationally and nationally renowned artists, who have been honored with prestigious awards.

Shree lal Joshi Kalyan Joshi Gopal Joshi are the renowned artists of Joshi family.
